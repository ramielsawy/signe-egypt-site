Meet
Signe Egypt

Signe Egypt is an Egyptian company and basically reflects an organization that is active on national, interstate and global levels with respect to international political economy or more specifically international trade and different types of international business activities as well as international relations interactions in general.

Our company represents a brand new engagement and orientation in the sphere of nowadays globalized world. Our main aim is to disseminate a brand new light at the global political economy and to make possible diverse types of relations between different countries, global transnational corporations, small and medium businesses as well as independent individual entrepreneurs.

Considering the fact that global political economy is becoming more and more complex thanks to the globalization and that different types of international business relations require additional assistance it is possible to notice that a high demand for consulting, advising and facilitating services in the arena of international relations and specifically international trade has come into life.

Thus, one of the main purposes for establishing our company was mainly a response to this very high demand for consulting, advising and lobbying services on international, but also on some national levels with respect to international trade, different inter-state relations as well as relations between various transnational corporations and independent individual enterprises as well.

Our international relations consulting services represent an accumulation of relevant experience, extensive knowledge and required skills applicable for these kinds of global and national activities.

Our Services

1. International Relations Consulting, Lobbying and Advising Our company provides lobby services on your behalf at different types of clients such as governments, transnational corporations, medium and small businesses, individuals and other entities of relevance to your needs.  We also provide appropriate consulting and advice services to our respected clients regarding different international relations issues and affairs such as political, social, economic, etc.
2. Trade, Finance and Insurance Global - National Brokers We also act as (inter)national trade brokers. We connect buyers and sellers (importers/exporters) all over the world and act on their behalf representing their interests in the process of various (inter)national transactions. Furthermore, we are in a position to ensure appropriate financial and insurance means to our clients necessary to start and complete different types of inter (national) transactions.
3. Investment Promotion Agents Signe Egypt provides a number of various promotional services to any entity (state, company, organization etc.) which is interested in investing or attracting investment anywhere in the world. We are also able to organize promotional, marketing or advertising services for our clients who would like to attract investment or invest in any part of the world.
